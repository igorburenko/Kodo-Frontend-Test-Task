Domestic and family violence: Focusing on perpetrators and perpetration

This website was funded by the Australian Government’s Department of Social Services through Australia’s National Research Organisation for Women’s Safety (ANROWS) as part of a broader project aimed at developing a detailed understanding of Australian perpetrator intervention systems (PI systems).

The broader report incorporates the range and breadth of PI systems responses, the most common pathways of identification, assessment and intervention for perpetrators, as well as identifying opportunities to strengthen PI systems and perpetrator accountability.

The report includes a discussion of the concept of PI systems, as well as the methodology, findings and recommendations relating to this site.

Prepared by

Dr Karen Upton-Davis, Chief Investigator, Curtin University
